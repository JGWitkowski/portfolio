Jim Fenner Golf Professional

Jim was introduced to golf at age 7 while learning, caddying, and playing at Worcester Country Club. He now spends most of his time teaching the game of golf to new and experienced players.

Below are several of Jim's current involvements:

  • Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI): Golf Coach
  • Massachusetts Golf Association: Golf Instructor – Junior Golf Program
  • Worcester Public Schools Night Life: Instructor-Intro to Golf
  • Cyprian Keyes Golf School: Golf Instructor
  • Worcester Public Schools: Golf 20:20 Golf Coordinator
  • Auburn Golf Driving Range: Golf Instructor
